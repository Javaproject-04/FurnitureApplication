Shipping Policy

FurnishFusion delivers across India. We strive to get your order to you safely and on time.

Delivery Time

Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days from order confirmation. Delivery times may vary by location and product availability.

Shipping Cost

We offer free shipping on all orders. No minimum order value is required.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive updates via email. You can also track your order status from the Orders page in your account.

Delivery Process

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days.
  • You will receive a notification when your order ships.
  • Our delivery partner will contact you to schedule delivery.
  • Please ensure someone is available to receive and inspect the delivery.

Damaged or Missing Items

If your order arrives damaged or items are missing, please contact us within 48 hours with photos. We will arrange a replacement or refund as appropriate.
