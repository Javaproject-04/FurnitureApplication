At FurnishFusion, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we're here to help.

How to Return

  • Contact us at Contact Us with your order number and reason for return.
  • We will provide return instructions and, if applicable, a pickup schedule.
  • Ensure the item is securely packed in its original packaging.
